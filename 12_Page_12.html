Feeling a pain as if biting my side, I raised myself. It was a snake. When I arrived at the place led by the snake, a pair of ropes appeared. The rope was hanging from a pulley located at the top of the tower. It was something I had never seen before. When I picked one and grabbed it, the rope turned into a snake and tied my body together. The pulley began to move smoothly. I felt my body slowly moving away from the ground. Something was coming down from the top of the tower tied to a rope. I could tell at once what it was.
